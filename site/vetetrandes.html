Vetetrandes is the remains of a city where Yajnev rests.

As you would approach the epicenter of the bound city, time would become increasingly more unmoving.

At its center, one would find the corpse of yajnev, and where Yajnev's head would have been, a hollow mask. Locked inside the mask is the only instance of true space that can be said to be "outside". Vetetrandes is located between Risan Aldeth and Clionn, on the southern hemisphere of Dinaisth.

The trek across Vetetrandes is only possible through the guiding of an Actor, as the local void existing in Vetetrandes is unacted, or unoperated and unredered.

The Neon Hermetists believe this location to be an ancient and broken soies_machine.

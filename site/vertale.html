    Vertale is the equatorial region of Dinaisth.

    A gravitational anomaly makes structures temporarily weightless when Aitasla's orbit is parallel with the equator of Dinaisth. The combination of the oscillating downward pull and gale-force winds have created a terrain very hard to navigate. The Vertale islands are filled with the debris from the tunneling machines that once operated in Es.

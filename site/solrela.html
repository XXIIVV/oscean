    Sudre's original categorization of words by first syllable is altogether incompatible with its concept of reversibility and too arbitrary to be used consistently, but to preserve compatibility with other dialects, the ternary dictionary has been left mostly untouched. In time, disused words will become overloaded with modern meanings.

    Drownspire was a small studio run in collaboration with Rekka between 2010 to 2012.

    We designed, produced and sold art projects. We made art toys, jewlery, apparel and sold tickets for chiptune shows in Montreal.

    We closed the studio after we decided to move to Tokyo. Drownspire was the precursor to Hundred Rabbits. We distributed the Vambits designer toys and published the Merure books.

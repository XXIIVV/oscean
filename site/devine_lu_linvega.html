56.

Co-founder of Hundred Rabbits and full-time vagabond, currently drifting somewhere along the foggy coast of the Pacific North West. I enjoy living on the water, moving with the seasons, growing food in places I'm not supposed to, and reading by the woodstove when it rains.

Since 2006, I've been populating this wiki with notes on various topics, including languages, discourse and technology. This living document chronicles my exploration into finding ways for my nomadic and creative projects to co-exist; so that I can keep composing experimental music, illustrating these fictional worlds, and writing esoteric software.

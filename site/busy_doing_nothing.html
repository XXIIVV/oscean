    The logbook of our Pacific Ocean crossing from Japan to Canada.

    In 2020, we spent 51 days at sea during the sailing from Japan to Canada, it was the hardest thing we had ever done. We kept a physical logbook of daily happenings onboard. This is that logbook, revised, and with a ton of extra notes, recipes and illustrations.

    The book is the daily ship's log written by Rekka, intertwined with things we learnt, recipes and thoughts from the previous 6 years of sailing around the Pacific Ocean aboard Pino.

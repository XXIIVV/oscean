    Thousand Rooms is a visual novel.

    Thousand Rooms was created in collaboration with illustrator Rekka Bellum, documenting the short vignettes of Bat, Cat, Owl and Fox. We have written this book with the hopes of creating a sort of Borges for children, in which the reader follows four characters who try and make sense of their small world.

    The book also encourages to try and understand the rules, and plan unsuggested avenues. We are releasing the book in English, French, Russian, Japanese & Solresol.

    Vambits are small DIY desktoys designed to be laser cut in acrylic.

    The critters were initially created as Drownspire, in collaboration with Ponoko but is now produced by our friends at Skógr Studio.

    The templates are free and open-source if you want to create your own faces and custom accessories! Send us your pictures if you do.

    The design was later stolen and sold by various resellers on Esty and designer-toys boutiques in Australia.

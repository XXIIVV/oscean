    Ver'Iytsl

    Neonev's northward glider trip from Vetetrandes over the Eaurison isles and westward over to the Es plains. The cover shows an Aitaslan(Oylaen), with the red aldewear that Neonev sometimes has on in space, entering an Injection Light. If the injection light look like a fiber of Paradichlorisse, it's by design.

    Known Magye is a Laeisthic album telling the tales of the arrival of Andes in Dinaisth

    Sometimes reminiscent of the compositions of Children of Bramble, this album is a trip across the history of Andes' arrival, one of the versions of the cover for this album features the Known Magye, one of the sculptures found in the Andes Castel.

    This single is the second part of the laeisthic collection, and was recorded live from San Fransisco's POW POW POW which was controlled entirely with the Leap Motion.

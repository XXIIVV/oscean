    Ayatori is a x250 Thinkpad.

    It presently runs the community version of Manjaro(i3), the laptop was donated to Hundred Rabbits by Josh Auget. It is my coastal workstation, as it's a bit too power hungry for our current solar setup. During the summer months, during which we are often in transit, I tend to favor Usagi.

    ayatori specs

    • OS: Manjaro 20.1.1 Mikah
    • Resolution: 1366x768
    • WM: i3
    • Font: Noto Sans 10
    • Disk: 114G
    • CPU: Intel Core i5-5200U @ 4x 2.7GHz
    • GPU: Intel(R) HD Graphics 5500 (BDW GT2)
    • RAM: 7660MiB

Wiktopher is a fiction novel.

Wiktopher, written by Rek Bell, tells the stories of Lupen and Eka, as they sandfin across the Soronan Desert and learn about the varied lives of its inhabitants. The book features illustrations of creatures, plants, maps, games and languages of the different cultures of its world. Themes include cultural identity, food preservation, community, low-tech and more.

It was written while circumnavigating the Pacific Ocean by sail, and over many summers in the Salish Sea. It was written by Rek Bell, and edited by Devine Lu Linvega. The writing is gender neutral and should be appropriate for children of 10 and up.

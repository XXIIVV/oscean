    Goals must have an explicit state of completion and a specified deadline.

    Internal are goals dealing with personal growth, and external, as having an effect on the outside world.

    GoalStatus
    Internal Targets
    See the world. Travel the earth by my own means and my own terms. Find countries too small to be seen on a map and go there. Watch the northern lights from the sea. Currently living in Victoria, after effectively completing a 4 years long sailing adventure circumnavigating the Pacific Ocean, across countries I did not know existed.

    Started dreaming of seeing the north, began preparing the vessel for northern weather, sailed to Alaska.
    Become polyglot. Speak four languages from four different roots. Acquired a basic understanding of written and spoken casual Japanese after living in Japan for 4 years. Took introductory Russian classes.
    Get in shape. Lower resting heart rate, increase lower and upper body strength. An hour and a half workout, every second day. A routine of 200cal/15min horizontal bicycle, legpress, benchpress, kettle bells and hanging leg raise.
    External Targets
    Inspire an interest in arts & sciences through tooling and mentorship. Assemble a collective of artists and developers capable of becoming multi-disciplinarists. Created a collection of free and open-source tools to compose music, to create graphics and to write. Currently building decentralized or federated social tools to fuel collaboration among the Merveilles collective.
    Grow an interest into sustainability and resilience, especially aimed at minizing the pain and suffering of non-human animals through experiments and direct action. Currently living and working off-the-grid, powered by solar energy, have optimized to generate the least amount of waste through a somewhat ascetic lifestyle and a plant-based diet. Expecting to work on adaptation through repairability in the near future, and improve documentation and replicability of experiments.
    Success through smallness.

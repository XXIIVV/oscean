    Ann is an assembly syntax for symmetric interaction combinators(SIC).

    An advanced civilisation lands a ship on earth, they have never developed an alphabet, the pen-n-paper game sprouts is their most competitive activity, the ship's computer is programmed entirely by little scribbles of nodes that reorganize themselves based on specific rules.

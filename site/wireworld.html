    Wireworld is a turing-complete cellular automaton that resembles simulating wires and transistors.

    Wireworld uses what is called the Moore neighborhood, which means that in the rules above, neighboring means one cell away in any direction, both orthogonal and diagonal.

    Rules

    1. head, becomes tail
    2. tail, becomes conductor
    3. conductor, becomes head with 1 or 2 heads neighbors.

Spent the last few days between Bregenz and Hohenems, in Austria, for the Ultramateria festival. I was invited to talk about the Hundred Rabbits design philosophy and take part in a round-table discussion on the topic of Utopias.

On saturday, I gave an Orca workshop to a crowd of people, including students from the CIM Lab in Ravensburg, and played some techno in the gorgeous Collini Areal building. I also took this opportunity to meet with local activists fighting for fair wages for artists, get lost in the train system, wander up snowy hills at night and practice my non-existent German.

  • Improved Orca's interface.
  • Implemented a tug-o-war memory model for Drifblim.
  • Did a brief appearance on ORF's Vorarlberg Heute Vom.

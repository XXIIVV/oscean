They live deeply these hlessil

From glass rectangles, the hrair eyes of the homba peer down at the tiny ship rocked in its violent wake. The hrududu leaves a suffocating, oily embleer as it sails past them, tharn with terror, they glare back.

After journeying northward for a long time, Dander and Freckle look back and at last, the elil are now far behind. They've made their way into a quiet bay where the hrududu dare not come, they drop a heavy metal hook over the side for they know that before them lay the narn days, endless and warm.

They spend delightful hours under the golden radiance of Frith, rowing to the dense wild woods, wandering its overgrown trails, occasionally stopping to silflay. When the light wanes, they tell stories under the light of Inlé.

They live deeply, these hlessil.

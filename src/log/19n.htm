Polycat

Spent a few days making Polycat with Rek for Catjam 2025. It was a nice distraction from the Uxn book and an opportunity for us to make a little platformer together. We worked on the game during our sail up the west coast of Vancouver Island on our way to Ucluelet. We had a few days of downtime along the way, when we were anchored and surrounded by hummingbirds, making sure that our strange little cat could make it over the obstacles.

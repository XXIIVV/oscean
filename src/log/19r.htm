Year Of The Basic Desktop!

Once again, I find myself wasting valuable scientific computing equipment on clerical work. This time around, I've persuaded my computer to evaluate BASIC programs. I began implementing the graphical interpreter back in 2023 without ever finishing it, leaving it in an utterly unusable state.

But, I resumed working on it the other day, overcome by a kind of creative fever, I finished it, released it as Sunflower BASIC and I haven't been able to put it down since. It's just too fun. Maybe this will appeal to someone's else nihilistic programming sensibilities!

I beg your parsnips?!

You have one of those morbid friends who wear only black, who write only in Forth and that modern times have all but left to linger, undead among the coaxial cables. They're inviting you. Dead languages, dead systems, aren't they just infinitely precious, once you realize that the ideas that survive are never the interesting ones, merely those with the most capital behind them.

Doesn't that just make the graveyard suddenly burst with life and reveal the modern computing landscape for what it is. A mausoleum, a putrid stench, a crusty gold plaque to the memory of the rotting fascist who would entomb the world below highways of microplastics.

Won't you come instead? And delight among the living discarded ferment!

Gaud!

Gaud es ling de programation concatenativ ub information es un sac de symbols et omn operation es composition de fractions per multiplication, de qui denominator consum symbols et numerator inser symbols.

The English version of this page is available here.

Fundamental

  • Stat de program: Sac de symbols sin ordin. [cub con^3] et [con^3 cub] contin un cub et tres con.
  • Symbols: Elements de sac es scrib ut numer decimal aut nomin pro numer prim.[8 sphaer^2] val [2^3 sphaer sphaer].
  • Transformation: Fraction denominator indic consumption et numerator insertion. strix/[fels^2] consum du fel, inser un strix.

Evaluation es aplication de fractions in sequent. Leg un fraction, et..

  • Si denominator es in sac:
    • Fraction es aplicabil, erg consum denominator et inser numerator.
    • Tent juxt fraction.
  • Si nul fraction reman:
    • Program sist.
[fel can ran^2]      ( Sac hab 1 fel, 1 can et 2 rans )
[fel can^3]/ran      ( Si sac hab 1 ran, camb pro 1 fel et 3 cans )
    []/[ran fel^2]   ( Si sac hab 1 ran et 2 fels, tol ils )
    urs/can^fel      ( Si sac hab tam mult can quam fel..
                        .. me camb can in numer de fel pro 1 urs )
[can^4 urs]          ( Me reman cum 4 cans et 1 urs )

Met

Met @nomin es special symbols, id retin position de salt in program. Quand met es in sac, evaluation salt ad position. Hic es exempl de un simpl circuit:

tempor^5

@Circuit ( tempor -- )
	Circuit/tempor
fin

Arithmetic

Pro praeb diferent inter 2 numer, program consum 1 de singul usq ad exhaustion, que relinq x pro event positiv, aut y pro event negativ. Ultim du met exhaur numer aut pos aut neg:

x^2 y^5

@Sub ( x y -- pos|neg )
	Sub/[x y] 'pos/x 'neg/y

[x^2 y^5] Sub/[x y] 'pos/x 'neg/y 
[x y^4] Sub/[x y] 'pos/x 'neg/y 
[y^3] Sub/[x y] 'pos/x 'neg/y 
[y^3] 'pos/x 'neg/y 
[y^3] 'neg/y

[neg^3] 'neg/y 
[neg^3]

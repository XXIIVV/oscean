From Saharaphorest is an album telling the tale of a visit at Paradichlorisse.

The album was recorded as it played through cassette tapes.The last one is a single clean mastered version. A handful of tapes were made and given to fans at shows as artworks.

This release is follows the Vert Kirlian Theatre album, released in 2008.

The cover features Neoneve with a Whiinders Mask.

The Camera specs.

For film, I use the Voigtländer's Bessa R3M 250 Jahre, my favourite black and white film is the Ilford DELTA 3200.

For digital, I use the Sony Alpha a6000 24.3 MP, I use Novoflex's NEX/LEM Adapter to fit the M-Mount lenses.

My lenses are the Voigtländer Super Wide Heliar 15mm f4.5, the Voigtländer Ultron 28mm f2, the Soligor Macro 28mm f2.8 and the Nikkor 50mm f1.2

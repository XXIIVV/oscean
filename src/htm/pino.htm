Pino is a Yamaha 33 sailboat built in 1982.

Purchased in 2016, on Vancouver Island, Pino has since sailed across the Pacific Ocean from Mexico to New Zealand, Fiji, Japan, Alaska and countless places inbetween.

If you wish to learn more, we maintain this list of technical details, and update our current position via the tracker. To learn more about living and working aboard a sailboat, see the Hundred Rabbits. A book documenting the 52 days Pacific Crossing from Japan to Canada is available here.

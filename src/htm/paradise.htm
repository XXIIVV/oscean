Paradise is an interactive-fiction playground.

In Paradise, the world is a sea of nodes, each with a single parent and any number of children. A user is a force acting upon the world through a became vessel. There are no division between "users", "files" and "applications", everything is a vessel.

library{library}*
	> ghost{ghost}

A vessel is an object that can act onto other objects, possibly carrying objects, with its own permissions. The lobby begins with a ghost created by itself inside a library created by itself, within itself. Upon creation, the ghost is the default user vessel. Vessels may be within themselves, or even organized in the shape of a loop.

Pockets of space can be hosts to vessels existing around a common purpose, or carried around inside another vessel as you would a tool-belt or an inventory, giving the acting vessel, or user, additional actions appended to the list of primitives.

You are a ghost in a teapot.
The inside of the teapot is stormy.

You see a cat.
You carry a haunted house.

The interface shows who is where, a note about the parent vessel when there is one, what is seen and what is carried.

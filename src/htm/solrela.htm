Ternary Solrela

The following common words are ordered according to the permutations of the Solfege, the original religious terms were replaced as per this document. There are three types of non-reversible words: words with repeated syllables used for counters, and palindromic words used for unknown and 3-times syllables for unknown.

Sutre's original categorization of words by first syllable is altogether incompatible with its concept of reversibility and too arbitrary to be used consistently, and so the original philosophical taxonomy of words is ignored in favor of the more predictable association of concepts by syllabic inversion.

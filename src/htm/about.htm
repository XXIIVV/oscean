This wiki is a digital playground and personal logging system.

Launched in 2008, this wiki was created to serve as a personal assistant for managing a vast collection of records, including journal logs, project notes, and curated pages of general knowledge.

This type of website is a often referred to as a Memex, a living document and archive of where one has been, and a tool for exploring where one might go next.

Consider a future device, a sort of mechanized private library in which an individual stores all his books, records, and communications, and which may be consulted with exceeding speed and flexibility. It is an enlarged intimate supplement to his memory. Vannevar Bush, As We May Think

Oscean is implemented in Uxntal and has a small virtual machine built to endure the accelerating pace of digital information decayas its only dependency. It is designed to evolve with my needs and adapt to the shifting landscape of modern technology.

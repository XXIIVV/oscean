This wiki is a digital playground and personal logging system.

Launched in 2008, this wiki was created to serve as a personal assistant for managing a vast collection of bi-monthly updates, journal logs, project notes, and curated pages of general knowledge.

Oscean is implemented in Uxntal with as only dependency a small virtual machine built to endure the accelerating pace of digital information decay. It is designed to evolve with my needs and adapt to the shifting landscape of modern technology.

Some notes are written in Latin Sin Flexion(Lø), it is a simplified dialect of Latin devised by the mathematician Giuseppe Peano. It should be possible to make sense of the sentences with only a little practice, otherwise, choose the (Lø) button in the footer to translate the page.

This type of website is a often referred to as a Memex, a living document and archive of where one has been, and a tool for exploring where one might go next.

Consider a future device, a sort of mechanized private library in which an individual stores all his books, records, and communications, and which may be consulted with exceeding speed and flexibility. It is an enlarged intimate supplement to his memory. Vannevar Bush, As We May Think

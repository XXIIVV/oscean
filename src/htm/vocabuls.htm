Vocabuls

A collection of words and expression to get started with Latin Sin Flexion.

Expressions

  • Heus, hello.
  • Io! Cert. Yeah! Certainly.
  • Etiam, even so.
  • It, true, so, yes.
  • Non, not so.
  • Nul mod! No way!
  • Quomod val te? How are you?

Questions

Questions will often begin with a question word and put the verb before the pronoun:

  • Qui es me? who am I?
  • Que hab te? what do you have?
  • Quar non? why not?
  • Qual ocasion? which occasion?
  • Quem vos e inven? whom did you find?
  • Quand vad te? when are you going?

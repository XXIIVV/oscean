Hiversaires is a point-n-click game.

Hiversaires is a text-less experimental game, like the isometric adventure game Oquonie, that lures the player through a series of meandering corridors. The game was originally released on iOS platforms, and re-released on all desktop platforms in 2018, thanks to Jeremy Sachs who also ported Verreciel.

The game also features an original soundtrack..

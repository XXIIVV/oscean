Polycat is a tough little platformer game.

Jump around a grey world populated with hard to reach platforms and a few birds that will lend you a hand. There are always birds in our games for some reason. This project was made in collaboration with Rek who did all the pretty assets and animations, locations in the world are written in Roudo Ses.

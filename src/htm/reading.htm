The reading list anthology.

The content of this list is not an endorsement of the views of the authors, or of their books, it is merely a record of which ones I have read over the year. Please be advised, most of these books are for discerning adults.

For a list of all time favourites, see directory. Stars next to book titles indicate a second reading, highlighted rows indicate that year's favourite.

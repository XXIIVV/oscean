Despite being from there, Canada has become a travel destination to me.

After spending 49 days at sea, sailing from Shimoda(Japan), we returned in Victoria(Canada), effectively completing a 5 years circumnavigation of the Pacific Ocean, via New Zealand. We have since sailed north from British Columbia to Alaska and back.

One option that reflects the geographical nature of British Columbia is the Chinook term Illaheechuk or Illahee Chuk, which means "where land meets water", where illahee (or il'-la-hie) means land, ground or earth and chuk means water.

  • chuck: water
  • il'-la-hie: Earth
There was not a single prospect that was pleasing to the eye.
